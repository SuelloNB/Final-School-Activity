PERSONAL JOURNEY

I am a Computer Engineering graduate with a strong interest in software development, embedded systems, and technology-driven problem solving. My academic and project experience has allowed me to work on solutions involving web applications, IoT systems, robotics, and interactive digital platforms. Through these experiences, I have developed a practical understanding of how technology can be designed and applied to address real-world needs.

Over time, my interest in technology has grown into a commitment to continuous learning and improvement. I enjoy exploring both the technical and practical sides of development, from understanding system behavior to building solutions that are functional, accessible, and user-centered. I am particularly motivated by opportunities that allow me to apply my skills, collaborate with others, and contribute to meaningful technology projects.

This website serves as a professional portfolio that highlights my background, projects, and growth as an aspiring technology professional. It reflects my dedication to learning, building, and continuously improving as I pursue opportunities in the field of software and technology.
