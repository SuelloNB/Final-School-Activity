NEWS

𝐌𝐚𝐲 𝟗, 𝟐𝟎𝟐𝟓 – The Engineering Department took part in the Chancellor’s Free Breakfast Program held at the Social Hall, Annex 2 Building. The distribution was supervised by esteemed faculty members, including Engr. Bubuli, Ms. Rivera, and the faculty’s working scholar. A total of 60 breakfast meals were distributed from 7:00 AM to 7:30 AM, with UCLM students actively participating in the initiative. This ongoing program continues to promote student wellness by offering a nutritious start to the day, supporting better focus and participation in academic activities.
